zuchini tart

In order to make this meal you need these ingredients:

  • 1kg of Zuchini
  • bread crums
  • 2 eggs
  • 100g butter
  • 1 kg of onion
  • 500g flour
  • 3g salt

Now lets make our zuchini tart

  1. Pour the content of the eggs inside some bowl and mix them with 500g of flour and the butter
  2. knead the preparation until it does not stick on your hands (dough)
  3. cut into very small pieces the zuchinis and the onions
  4. cook the vegetables in the stove
  5. take a mold pan and stretch the dough so that the surface is completely covered
  6. put the vegetables inside the dough
  7. bake the tart for 30 minutes
