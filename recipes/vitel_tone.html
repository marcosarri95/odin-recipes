Vitel toné

In order to make this meal you need these ingredients:

  • 1.5 kg of peceto (meat)
  • 2 onions
  • 4 anchovies
  • 500g of mayonnaise
  • 100g of tuna

Now lets make our zuchini tart

  1. boil the peceto for 1 hour
  2. cut the peceto into very thin pieces
  3. whit the help of a mixer, mix the anchovies, mayonnaise and tuna
  4. put the preparation above the peceto
