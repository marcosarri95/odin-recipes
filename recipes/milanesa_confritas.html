Milanesa with fry potatoes

In order to make this meal you need these ingredients:

  • 1 Chiken breast
  • bread crums
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 garlic
  • 1 parsley
  • 2 fresh potatoes (medium or big size
  • 1 bottle of cooking oil

Now lets make our Milanesa:

  1. Pour the content of the eggs inside some bowl
  2. cut into very small pieces 2 garlic theet and the parsley and pour them inside the bowl again
  3. put the chiken breast inside the preparation
  4. pass the chiken throug the bread crums
  5. fry the chiken until its completely cook
  6. cut the potatoes into canes shape
  7. fry them into cooking oil
